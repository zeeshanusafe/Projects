Web Check-in Information

Everything you need to know about online check-in for a smooth travel experience

Find Your Airline

Locate your airline from our list and click the "Check-in Now" button to be directed to their official web check-in page.

Enter Your Details

Provide your booking reference (PNR) and last name as they appear on your ticket or confirmation email.

Select Your Seat

Choose your preferred seat from the available options. Some airlines may charge extra for premium seats.

Get Your Boarding Pass

Download or email your boarding pass. You can also save it to your mobile wallet for easy access.

Check-in Timing

Online check-in typically opens:

  • 24-48 hours before departure for domestic flights
  • 24-72 hours before departure for international flights
  • Closes 2-4 hours before departure

Required Documents

Have these ready before starting web check-in:

  • Booking reference (PNR)
  • Last name as on booking
  • Passport (for international flights)
  • Visa documents (if required)

Airport Arrival

Even with web check-in, arrive at the airport with sufficient time:

  • Domestic flights: 2 hours before departure
  • International flights: 3 hours before departure
  • During peak seasons: Add extra 30-60 minutes

Special Cases

Web check-in may not be available for:

  • Unaccompanied minors
  • Passengers requiring special assistance
  • Group bookings (check with airline)
  • Certain international destinations

Save Time

Skip long queues at the airport check-in counters and head straight to security or baggage drop.

Digital Convenience

Access your boarding pass on your smartphone - no need to print unless required by your airline.

Seat Selection

Choose your preferred seat in advance - window, aisle, or extra legroom if available.

Update Details

Easily update contact information, add frequent flyer numbers, or make minor changes to your booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about web check-in

What do I need for web check-in?

You'll need your booking reference (also called PNR or confirmation code) and the last name of the passenger as it appears on the booking. For international flights, you may also need passport details.

Can I check in online if I have baggage to check?

Yes, you can still check in online if you have baggage to check. After online check-in, you'll need to visit the airline's baggage drop counter at the airport to hand over your luggage. This is usually faster than the regular check-in line.

What if I can't check in online?

If you're unable to check in online, it could be due to several reasons: special assistance requirements, group booking, visa or documentation issues, or technical problems. In such cases, proceed directly to the airport check-in counter with your travel documents.

Is web check-in available for all flights?

Most airlines offer web check-in for both domestic and international flights, but there may be exceptions for certain destinations or special circumstances. Some airlines may not offer online check-in for flights to/from specific countries due to security requirements.

Can I select or change my seat during web check-in?

Yes, most airlines allow you to select or change your seat during web check-in, though premium seats may require an additional fee. Seat availability is subject to the airline's policy and aircraft configuration.
