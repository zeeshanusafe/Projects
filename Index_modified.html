Luggage Information

Standard Baggage Allowances

Most airlines have similar baggage policies, but always check with your specific carrier for exact allowances.

Airline Type Cabin Bag Checked Baggage Weight Limit
Economy Class 1 piece 1 piece (may vary) 23kg (50lbs)
Premium Economy 1-2 pieces 2 pieces 23kg each
Business Class 2 pieces 2 pieces 32kg each
First Class 2 pieces 2-3 pieces 32kg each
Important Notes
  • Check-in luggage dimensions typically should not exceed 158cm (62 inches)
  • Cabin baggage usually limited to 55x40x20cm (22x16x8 inches)
  • Excess baggage fees can be substantial - check before you travel
  • Some items are prohibited in both cabin and checked luggage
Pro Tips
  • Weigh your luggage at home to avoid surprises at the airport
  • Pre-pay for extra baggage online to save up to 50%
  • Consider airline loyalty programs for extra baggage allowances
  • Pack essentials and valuables in your carry-on luggage
